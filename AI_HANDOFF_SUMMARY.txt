NES-TO-SEGA-GENESIS AI Handoff Summary
Date: 2026-03-12

Project goal
- Port Zelda 1 from NES to Sega Genesis while preserving the original game logic as much as possible.
- The project translates 6502 bank code into 68000 assembly, then emulates the NES PPU contract on top of the Genesis VDP.

What v226-v231 proved
- Broad "restore core intro data" changes do matter.
- But broad profiles are too coarse to tell which exact label is the real lever.
- v226 control, v227 core, v228 text, v229 core+text, v230 extended, and v231 full all remain on the same general intro lane.
- The biggest observable differences are:
  - the exact $0300 buffer family late in the run
  - whether legacy PPU fallback events wake up
  - which intro/story screenshots look closest to the real NES story screen
- That means the next best experiment is one-label-at-a-time isolation inside the "core" intro set.
